Our lager

A clean lager with golden depth.

Kakia Melissa Lager

Inspired by Vienna tradition, crafted with four select barley malts and two noble hops for a smooth, full-bodied amber with a golden glow and gentle floral finish. Balanced, elegant, and made to be shared under Kythiran skies.

  • Style: Vienna Lager
  • ABV: 5.0%
  • Taste: Velvety malt sweetness, subtle bread notes, gentle floral hop character with a crisp, dry finish.

Our story

A small brewery with big flavors.

Kakia Melissa, our lager, is named for an island legend—a symbol of warmth, wisdom, and bringing people together. Each batch honors that spirit: patient brewing, honest flavors, and a golden glass meant for sharing.

Slow brewing

Each batch is brewed with patience, allowing time for flavor to develop and the lager to settle.

Golden flavor

A delicate balance of malt sweetness and gentle hop bitterness, finished with a crisp, clean mouthfeel.

Friendly service

We welcome tastings, events and conversations about beer with honest Greek warmth.
