About Kakia Melissa

Handcrafted in every batch.

Kakia Melissa is a small brewery on Kythira, born from a couple's passion for good beer and Greek warmth. The name honors the island's legend of Kakia Melissa—a wise woman whose kindness brought people together. We brew lager the same way: with care, tradition, and the spirit of bringing good people around a table.
